When is the best time to grow tomatoes?

Posted By Noah Nasiali    on May 24, 2017   

I have been asked this question more times than I have been asked my name. Not because my name resonates with rain to be precise floods but due to the fact that I have planted tomatoes almost anytime of the year. But honestly does timing matter any more in farming?? What are the best times to plant what? When does the market prices ever favor farmers? My answer after eight years is it never does and it will never ever favor farmers.
