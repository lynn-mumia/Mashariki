Who We Are


Eteina is a social enterprise with the goal of helping farmers achieve their farming dreams and goals. We also offer farm management consultancy services. Besides this, we have a seedling planters department where we grow certified seeds into healthy and ready to plant seedlings.


Based on the actual tried and tested skills that we developed from the more than 10 years experience, we are confident that we will be of great help to farmers. We work with some of the highly skilled agronomists in ensuring that the information you receive is credible and applicable to you.
