Our careers

Agribusiness Sales Intern.Our client is an Agri-tech start-up company in Kenya working with smallholder farmers to improve production and increase efficiency using modern technologies.


Agribusiness Sales Internship Responsibilities

  • Create demand and brand awareness by product promotion, demonstration and trials.
  • Demonstrate the value of the company products and services
  • Establish and Maintain relationships with existing customers
  • Develop new prospects and interact with customers to increase sales of products.
  • Keep records and prepare reports on sales as required.
  • Maintain continuing education on marketing techniques, product and services.
  • Provide training to Greenhouse employees and customers as needed.
  • Represent the company at local and regional meetings and trade shows.
  • Research and respond to technical inquiries by consulting with internal and industry specialists in order to solve any potential issues or question.

Qualifications of the Agribusiness Sales Intern.

  • Bachelor degree in Agriculture or business-related field, or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Knowledge and experience in Agriculture knowledge of the crop/soil/ will be an added advantage.
  • Entry level agronomists are encouraged to apply Competencies
  • Management of farm records is an added advantage
  • Knowledge of Social media/digital platforms will be an added advantage
  • Highly developed customer service skills.
  • Ability to develop strong customer relationships.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skill.
  • Ability to work under minimum supervision.

Send 1 page resume, including 2 referees, scanned passport size photo, links to your Facebook, Linkedin, Twitter profiles to: hr@eteina.co.ke⁠⁠⁠⁠
